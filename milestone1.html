2007: Netflix Introduces Streaming Service

In 2007, Netflix introduced its streaming service, revolutionizing the way we consume media. This milestone marked the beginning of a new era in entertainment, where content could be accessed instantly over the internet.

Before the streaming service, Netflix was primarily known for its DVD rental service, which allowed customers to rent DVDs online and receive them by mail. The introduction of streaming significantly increased Netflix's customer base and transformed the company's business model.
